### What You'll Learn

Throughout this workshop series, you'll develop practical skills in:
- **Python Programming** for data analysis
- **Data Loading & Cleaning** techniques
- **Data Visualization** and exploratory analysis
- **Statistical Analysis** including correlations, p-values, and t-tests
- **Regression Analysis** for modeling relationships
- **Machine Learning** fundamentals and advanced applications

These skills are crucial for conducting rigorous research projects that involve data analysis and computational methods.
